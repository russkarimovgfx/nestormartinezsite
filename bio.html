Biography

From a very early age, inspired by his Brother, Latin Music, and RnB, Nestor started developing a passion for singing. Growing up very shy and reserved he wasn’t comfortable with the spotlight, singing was something he kept to himself until the age of 14.Throughout high school he worked on his craft and participated in all music, theatre, and community artistic events. At the age of 18 he started auditioning for open calls in New York City and at the age of 19 landed his first professional gig as a participant of the “Nueva Estrella Awards” Pilot for CBS. This opened the way to more opportunities like performing with the “Barrio Boyzz”, Singing Background Vocals For “Fragancia” of DLG, and Being part of a Music Video for “El General”. His hard work did not come unnoticed, through persistence and determination Nestor landed his first opportunity to be heard nationwide singing on “Making The Band For Menudo” on MTV 3. Nestor is known for his passionate performances and strong latin melodies. Largely influenced by Latin artists; Ricky Martin, Enrique Iglesias, And Marc Anthony and also with RnB Influences; Boyz II Men, Dru Hill, and Ginuwine. Inspired by songwriters and poets; Jon Secada, Baby Face, Pablo Neruda, etc.. At an early age Nestor found passion in writing poetry that soon started forming into his Songwriting. This became a very big part of his Artistry as he found ways to channel personal and vulnerable experiences with meaningful messages through his lyrics and music. Captivating a variety of wide ranging audiences, for over a decade he has been part of major acts in cruise ships, traveling theater, corporate events, and celebrity weddings. Not only is he a seasoned entertainer but he has been on Nationwide TV Commercials: Direct TV, SuperCuts that aired for the MLB. As well as Nationwide Billboards; Direct TV, and Buchanan that were also shown in the Dominican Republic. He can also be found on Print and Media Ads for Bud Light, Smirnoff, Walmart, Brothers Sew, and Modeling Campaign for Prep Descovy. In recent years Nestor has been shaping and developing his sound in the industry. He was inspired by his Spaniard roots and influenced by European Music. Which led him to film and record his first single “Fuego” produced by Kristian Gusho and Directed by Four Infinit Productions in the mesmerizing views of Vlore, Albania. His Recent Single “Cada Vez” Produced by Cris Cas and Directed By Adler Lafluer, is a representation that he is The New Wave of Latin Music Today. You can find his Latest Music and Videos on all Media Platforms; Spotify, iTunes, Amazon Music, Youtube, etc…
